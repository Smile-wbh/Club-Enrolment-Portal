User Guidelines
  • Registration must use a real and valid email address, and account credentials should be stored securely.
  • Only one booking is allowed per time slot to avoid wasting shared resources.
  • Bookings should be cancelled at least 2 hours in advance.
  • After 3 no-shows, booking access should be suspended for 7 days.
  • Forum posts and comments should remain respectful, with abusive or non-compliant content prohibited.
Club Registration Standards
  • Club names must be unique and should avoid misleading terms such as official.
  • Descriptions must be truthful and images must be compliant and clear. False promotion is not allowed.
  • Applications should be reviewed within 24 hours of submission.
Club Operations Rules
  • Owners should keep their club information up to date.
  • Cancelled activities should be communicated to all booked users in advance.
  • Do not abuse announcements or maliciously mark users as no-shows.
Admin Governance Rules
  • Reviews should be strict, fair, and neutral.
  • Violations may lead to content removal, mute actions, or account suspension.
  • Administrators must protect user privacy and personal information.
