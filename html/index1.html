About Us

View Clubs

The platform is built for campus and community users, offering one place to browse clubs, explore courses, book time slots, register for events, and receive updates. Members can quickly view club types, locations, opening hours, and remaining seats with less communication overhead.

For club managers, the site also supports onboarding maintenance, booking capacity settings, activity publishing, and forum interaction, so training schedules, event organisation, and member notices can all be handled in one place.
