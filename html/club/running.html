Running Club

Running Club supports regular group runs for members who want to build fitness, pacing, and consistency. Sessions include easy mileage, interval work, and social runs so members can train at a comfortable level and progress steadily.

Club Overview

The club creates a welcoming environment for building a regular running habit. Training can range from short easy runs to structured interval sessions, with pace groups and route guidance that help members stay comfortable and consistent.

Venue Information

  • Meet-up point: Usually near the main campus entrance or the agreed route start point.
  • Check-in: Runners gather around 10 minutes early to confirm groups and route details.
  • Pace groups: Sessions may be split into easy, tempo, and confidence-building groups.
  • Route note: Most runs use campus paths or nearby public routes with safe regroup points.
  • Weather plan: Strong weather may shorten the route or move the session to conditioning and mobility work.

What We Do

  • Easy runs: build routine, aerobic fitness, and confidence
  • Structured work: intervals, tempo segments, and pacing practice
  • Technique: posture, cadence, and warm-up habits
  • Community: group check-ins, challenge tracking, and supportive goals

Who It Is For

  • Beginners starting a regular running habit
  • Members improving pacing and endurance step by step
  • Runners who like social training with route guidance
  • Anyone wanting flexible weekly cardio with a club atmosphere

Run Plan (Sample)

  • Warm-up: mobility and light activation (8-10 minutes)
  • Main block: easy run, intervals, or tempo section (20-40 minutes)
  • Regroup: recovery jog and route check-in
  • Cooldown: walk, stretch, and next-session notes

Equipment & Notes

  • Recommended: running shoes, weather-appropriate layers, and water
  • Safety: tell the organiser if you feel unwell or need an easier pace group
  • Courtesy: stay aware on shared paths and respect public spaces
  • Timing: arriving early helps with group pacing and route explanation
