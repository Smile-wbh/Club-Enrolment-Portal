Club Overview

The club aims to improve technique, fitness, and teamwork through structured sessions. New members can start with fundamentals and gradually move into more competitive drills and match play while keeping safety and fair play central.

Venue Information

  • Main venue: Outdoor Pitch A with marked training lanes and group warm-up space.
  • Check-in: Meet at the pitch entrance roughly 10 minutes before the session for sign-in and bib collection.
  • Changing and storage: Nearby changing space may be available depending on the venue schedule; personal bags are also common.
  • Pitch rule: Follow organiser instructions and avoid reckless tackles or unsafe challenges.
  • Weather plan: Heavy rain or strong wind may move the session to indoor conditioning or tactical review.

What We Do

  • Fundamentals: passing, first touch, dribbling, and finishing
  • Game drills: 2v2, 3v3, or 5v5 small-sided training
  • Tactics: shape, support play, and defensive responsibility
  • Match play: internal games and friendly fixtures

Who It Is For

  • Beginners learning the basics and match rules
  • Regular players maintaining fitness and improving decision-making
  • Members interested in team events and friendly competition
  • Anyone who enjoys outdoor group sport and teamwork

Training Plan (Sample)

  • Warm-up: light jog and dynamic mobility (10 minutes)
  • Core drills: passing patterns and ball control work (20-30 minutes)
  • Focused block: finishing, set plays, or wide combinations (20-30 minutes)
  • Game play: small-sided matches plus short review (30-40 minutes)

Equipment & Notes

  • Recommended: football boots or suitable trainers, shin guards, towel, and water
  • Safety: mention any knee or ankle issue before training so intensity can be adjusted
  • Fair play: respect teammates, organisers, and opponents during games
  • Timing: early arrival helps with check-in, grouping, and warm-up
