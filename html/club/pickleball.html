Pickleball Club

Pickleball Club offers approachable court sessions that mix basic strokes, serving, doubles movement, and social match play. The club is especially friendly for new members who want a fast-learning racket sport in a relaxed setting.

Club Overview

Pickleball sessions are designed to be welcoming and easy to join. Members work on technique, court awareness, and doubles rhythm while keeping the atmosphere social and low-pressure.

Venue Information

  • Main venue: Shared indoor court setup with temporary pickleball lines and nets.
  • Check-in: Arrive 10 minutes early to sign in and be assigned to a court group.
  • Equipment: Club paddles and balls may be available in limited numbers; bringing your own paddle is helpful.
  • Court etiquette: Rotate fairly after games and keep the non-playing area clear.
  • Venue updates: Court adjustments will be announced through club notices when needed.

What We Do

  • Basics: serve, return, dinks, and controlled volley contact
  • Movement: doubles positioning and transition to the kitchen line
  • Tactics: simple rally patterns and point construction
  • Play: ladder games, short matches, and partner rotation

Who It Is For

  • Beginners who want a quick and social way into racket sports
  • Members improving control, footwork, and doubles communication
  • Players looking for lighter but regular weekly activity
  • Anyone who enjoys structured games with a friendly atmosphere

Session Plan (Sample)

  • Warm-up: mobility and paddle-ball feel (10 minutes)
  • Skill block: serving, returns, and soft-shot drills (20-25 minutes)
  • Movement block: doubles spacing and transition play (20-25 minutes)
  • Games: rotating doubles matches with feedback (25-35 minutes)

Equipment & Notes

  • Recommended: court shoes, paddle if available, towel, and water
  • Safety: call balls clearly and avoid crossing into another active court
  • Courtesy: rotate partners and opponents fairly during shared sessions
  • Timing: early arrival helps with court assignments and warm-up
